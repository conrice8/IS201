Seeking a Community?

Utah County Canyoneering (UCC) is a community seeking to provide a place for local canyon enthusiasts to meet. We started in 2024 and have been slowly gaining popularity in Utah County. We have partipants as old as 76 and as young as 12!

What is Canyoneering?

Canyoneering is the technical ascent and descent of canyons. It involves hiking, rapelling, swimming, climbing and any activity that involves getting from point A to point B! Utah is full of places like these; Zion National Park, Bryce Canyon National Park, Moab. Fewer people know about the places right here in their backyard!

Locations

We started this website to help educate others on the many places here in Utah County that they can practice rapelling. These places include Fifth Water, Dry Canyon, Battle Creek, Bridal Veil and more! Join for the complete list.
